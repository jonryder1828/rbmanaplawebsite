Savings Accounts

Start saving today with our secure and high-interest savings accounts.

Why Choose Our Savings Accounts?

  • Competitive interest rates to grow your savings faster.
  • No hidden fees or charges.
  • Easy access to your funds through online and mobile banking.
  • Secure and reliable banking services.
  • Flexible account options to suit your needs.

Types of Savings Accounts

Basic Savings Account

Perfect for individuals who want to start saving with minimal requirements.

  • Minimum deposit: ₱500
  • No maintaining balance
  • Free online banking

High-Interest Savings Account

Earn higher interest rates with a larger deposit.

  • Minimum deposit: ₱10,000
  • Maintaining balance: ₱5,000
  • Free ATM card

Youth Savings Account

Designed for young savers to build good financial habits.

  • Minimum deposit: ₱100
  • No maintaining balance
  • Free passbook

How to Open a Savings Account

  1. Visit any of our branches with a valid ID and proof of address.
  2. Fill out the account opening form.
  3. Make an initial deposit.
  4. Receive your account details and start saving!

You can also start the process online by contacting us.
