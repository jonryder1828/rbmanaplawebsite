Credit Services

Access credit facilities tailored to your financial needs.

Why Choose Our Credit Services?

  • Low-interest rates for personal and business loans.
  • Flexible repayment options to suit your budget.
  • Quick and easy application process.
  • Transparent terms and conditions.
  • Dedicated customer support to guide you.

Types of Credit Services

Personal Loans

Get the funds you need for personal expenses, such as medical bills, education, or travel.

  • Loan amount: Up to ₱500,000
  • Interest rate: 6% per annum
  • Repayment term: 12 to 60 months

Business Loans

Finance your business expansion, equipment purchase, or working capital needs.

  • Loan amount: Up to ₱5,000,000
  • Interest rate: 7% per annum
  • Repayment term: 12 to 120 months

Agricultural Loans

Support your farming or agribusiness with affordable credit options.

  • Loan amount: Up to ₱1,000,000
  • Interest rate: 5% per annum
  • Repayment term: 6 to 36 months

How to Apply for Credit

  1. Visit any of our branches with the required documents.
  2. Fill out the loan application form.
  3. Submit proof of income, valid ID, and other necessary documents.
  4. Wait for approval and receive your funds.

You can also start the process online by contacting us.
