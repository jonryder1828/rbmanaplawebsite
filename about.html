About Rural Bank of Manapla

Serving the community for over 50 years.

RURAL BANK OF MANAPLA, INC. (RBM), was established in the year 1986 and started its operations in Manapla, Negros Occidental. For over 50 years, we have been serving clients not only in the municipality of Manapla but also in various towns across Negros.

We are committed to serving the financial needs of our rural community. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to providing accessible banking solutions, fostering economic growth, and supporting local businesses.

Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and businesses by offering tailored financial services that meet their unique needs. We believe in building strong relationships with our clients and contributing to the development of our community.
