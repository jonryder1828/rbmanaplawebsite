About Rural Bank of Manapla

Established in 1986, the Rural Bank of Manapla (RBM) has been a cornerstone of financial stability and growth in the rural community. Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and businesses by offering tailored financial solutions that meet their unique needs.

With a focus on community development, we provide a range of services including savings accounts, credit services, and loan programs designed to support local growth and prosperity.
